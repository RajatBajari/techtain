Problem Statement

The second largest country in the world, India, is home to approximately 63 million people in the deaf and hard-of-hearing community. Disabled people face many difficulties and are not able to get aware of what is going on in their surroundings, and it becomes difficult for them,heavier side of the problems is the deaths which occur. In day-to-day life, deaf people face many issues even from the ringing of the doorbell to that of food that is cooked. A deaf person needs to be notified of what is going on in his surroundings

Our Product

  • A deaf band which will help to notify the person while the bell rings
  • When the food is cooked and other daily activities
  • The mood of the used can be detected
  • Cardiac arrest can be detected
  • Band will undergo updation after fixed duration of time according to feedback given by the user
  • WHEN SOMEONE COMES ON THE DOOR THEN BAND WILL GIVE SIGNAL TO DEAF PERSON .

App Description

  • App in the mobile of family member helps them to be aware of the notification in the band of deaf person
  • Sensor captures the sound of doorbell and alongwith displaying that in screen of band it will also notify in the app too
  • App will have group of deaf person with doctors for communication
  • App will track and store the health data so that doctor can observe it
  • After free trial app will run on subscription basis to generate revenue

Description of software in band

Band will perform all the basic job of simple watch too
Will give the notification on screen once it detects the sound of doorbell or fire alarm
For children the graphical interface of software interacts with them easily
Software can integrate with different devices
Software is scalable and can function on different OS
Band will have specific code so that sensor detecting sound can send signal for notification to correct band
